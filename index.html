Business Idea Development Journal

Welcome to your digital business idea journal. This tool will guide you through the process of developing your business idea from concept to pitch-ready presentation. Follow each step carefully and document your thoughts and progress.

Getting Started

This journal consists of 8 essential steps:

  1. Identify a Problem Worth Solving
  2. Brainstorm and Pick an Idea
  3. Understand Your Customer
  4. Define Your Unique Value Proposition
  5. Design and Test Your Solution
  6. Create a Simple Business Plan
  7. Practice Your Pitch
  8. Deliver Your Pitch

Each step includes:

  • Clear instructions on what to do
  • Space for your ideas and notes
  • Tips to guide you
  • Expected outputs
